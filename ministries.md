## Cell Groups & Discipleship
Most PLCMM members belong to cell groups where they find community, spiritual growth, and practical support. We also run regular discipleship courses to equip leaders and facilitate a deeper understanding of the Christian faith.

## Children’s Ministry (Mighty Power Kids - MPK)
MPK combines engaging worship, bible stories, and small-group teaching to minister to children aged 4 to 12. MPK seeks to support children academically and spiritually with fun, age-appropriate programs and activities.

## Youth Ministry
Our youth ministry provides a safe, encouraging space for teenagers to grow in faith, develop leadership, and form lifelong Christian friendships. We focus on Bible study, mentorship, and serving together.

## Community Tuition Program
Free tuition is offered to students from the Lorong Ah Soo community to meet the students’ academic needs, as well as to build relationships with their families.

## Outreach Activities
Outreach is at heart of PLCMM’s mission. We connect with the Lorong Ah Soo community through our tuition ministry, festival celebrations, and home visits. Through sharing God’s love in practical ways, our programs have introduced many families to Christ over the years.

## Overseas Missions
PLCMM has participated in projects in Inner Mongolia, China and Prey Veng, Cambodia since 2005. PLCMM mission teams have helped to run church camps, dug wells, built homes, among other projects – going out by faith to share God’s love.
