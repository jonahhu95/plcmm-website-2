Our Community

We are a small multi-generational community church committed to following Jesus, caring for one another, and serving the Lorong Ah Soo community. Join us for worship services, cell groups, and outreach activities — everyone is welcome.

Worship Services

Our worship services are held every Sunday in both Mandarin and English. Our worship blends modern music, prayer, and dance — to honour God and invite others into His presence.

Service Timings

Mandarin Service: Sundays, 10:45 AM @ Naomi Hall

English Service: Sundays, 10:45 AM @ Performing Arts Studio (4th Floor)
