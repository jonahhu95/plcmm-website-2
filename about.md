## Our Story
Paya Lebar Chinese Methodist Missions (PLCMM) began in January 1999 as a faithful response to God’s call. With the support of Paya Lebar Chinese Methodist Church (PLCMC), what started with 25 people meeting in a converted school hall has grown into a vibrant mission outpost focused on worship, discipleship, and community outreach.

## Our Mission
We share the gospel with students and families from Paya Lebar Methodist Girls’ School (Primary) and residents from the Lorong Ah Soo neighbourhood through worship, tutoring, and outreach events.
