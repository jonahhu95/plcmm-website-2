## Our History
Paya Lebar Chinese Methodist Mission (PLCMM) has a rich history of serving the community in Singapore. We are part of the Methodist Church in Singapore, dedicated to spreading the Gospel and serving those in need.

## Our Vision
To be a lighthouse of God's love in Paya Lebar and beyond.

## Our Beliefs
We believe in the Holy Trinity, the authority of the Scriptures, and the transforming power of Jesus Christ.
